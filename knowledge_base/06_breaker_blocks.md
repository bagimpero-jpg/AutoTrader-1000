# Concept: Breaker Blocks (BB) & The Triple-Threat Framework

## Definition
A Breaker Block is a "Failed Order Block" — a zone where the market trend shifted, turning a previous supply zone into demand (or vice versa). It is the PRIMARY execution signal on the LTF.

## Identification Rules
- **Bullish Breaker**: Previous Bearish OB (last green candle before down move) that FAILS — price closes above it. On retest, acts as support for buy entry.
- **Bearish Breaker**: Previous Bullish OB (last red candle before up move) that FAILS — price closes below it. On retest, acts as resistance for sell entry.
- **Candle Close Requirement**: Only valid once a candle CLOSES outside the zone. A wick through does NOT count.

## The Triple-Threat Execution Framework
Must follow this EXACT 3-step sequence. Never trade a Breaker Block in isolation.
1. **Step 1 — HTF Bias (The Compass)**: Determine direction on Daily or 4H.
2. **Step 2 — HTF Level Tap (The Trigger Zone)**: Wait for price to hit untested HTF OB or FVG.
3. **Step 3 — LTF Breaker Block (The Execution)**: Switch to aligned LTF, identify the last candle that tried to continue the old trend but was "broken." Enter on retest.

## Timeframe Alignment Table
- Monthly/Weekly → Daily Market Structure
- Daily → 1-Hour Breaker Block
- 4-Hour → 15-Minute Breaker Block
- 1-Hour → 5-Minute Breaker Block
- 15-Minute → 1-Minute Breaker Block (Scalp Only)

## Entry Rules
- Entry on BB retest ONLY if nested inside an HTF POI.
- An LTF Breaker forming outside an HTF level has ~80% failure rate.
- "Let the market come to the zone" — if market goes straight to target without forming a BB, stay flat.

## Exit Rules
- TP at HTF External Liquidity (Old Highs/Lows or EQH/EQL).
- SL behind the Breaker Block zone or most recent LTF structural protection level.
- **One-Time Use**: Most effective on First Tap.

## Confluence Factors
- Nested inside HTF POI (OB/FVG/Demand zone).
- Preceded by liquidity sweep of session range.
- AMD phase alignment (distribution phase).

## Session Context
- LTF Breaker Blocks form during London and NY sessions.
- Best setups: London sweep of Asian levels → LTF BB → NY expansion.

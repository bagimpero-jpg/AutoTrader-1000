# Concept: Order Blocks (OB) & A+ Validation

## Definition
An Order Block is a zone where institutional "Big Money" placed significant orders, causing a trend shift. It's the last opposite-color candle before an impulsive structural break.

## Identification Rules
- **Bullish OB**: Last bearish (red) candle before a significant impulsive move UP that breaks a structural high.
- **Bearish OB**: Last bullish (green) candle before a significant impulsive move DOWN that breaks a structural low.
- **Marking Rule**: Mark the ENTIRE candle (highest wick to lowest wick) — the full region is the Zone of Interest.

## A+ Validation Rules (High-Probability Filter)
### Scenario A: The Range/Trap (AMD Logic) — 80-90% probability
- After OB forms, market should create a Range (consolidation) before returning to the OB.
- The range generates liquidity (stop losses) above and below.
- Bot waits for market to "sweep" range liquidity by tapping into the OB.
- **CRITICAL**: If price returns to OB via a "V-shape" (fast move, no range) → AVOID the trade (too much opposing momentum).

### Scenario B: The Liquidity Sweep (Inducement)
- The impulsive move that created the OB must have FIRST swept a previous major liquidity point (Old High or Old Low).
- This confirms Smart Money already cleared out retail traders.
- **If OB formed WITHOUT a preceding liquidity sweep** → mark as "Low Probability."

## Operational Constraints
- **One-Time Use**: Once price taps and reacts, orders are "filled." Delete the zone — do NOT trade 2nd/3rd tap.
- **Imbalance Requirement**: Valid OB should have an FVG immediately following the OB candle (proves impulsive institutional move).

## Entry Rules
1. Find A+ Order Block on HTF (1H or 4H).
2. Wait for price to enter the OB zone.
3. Switch to LTF (1m or 5m) and wait for CHoCH/Breaker Block.
4. Enter on LTF retracement with 1:2 RR minimum.

## Exit Rules
- TP at HTF External Liquidity.
- SL behind the OB zone or LTF protected level.

## Confluence Factors
- Range/consolidation before retest (Scenario A) = highest probability.
- Prior liquidity sweep (Scenario B) = confirmed institutional intent.
- FVG immediately after OB candle = institutional imbalance proof.
- OB + FVG overlap = A++ setup.

## Session Context
- HTF OBs identified on Daily/4H during all sessions.
- Execution only during London/NY on LTF confirmation.
